Wear a mask

tips:

  • Keep extra masks in your car/purse!
    • Even if you forget to bring a mask with you, you will always have one on hand
  • Although masks can feel stuffy and uncomfortable, you can brighten up the experience by designing masks that have cute designs

Avoid large gatherings when possible.

  • COVID-19 can spread rapidly in big groups. One person who has COVID-19 can spread it to everyone nearby.
  • If you are hosting events like weddings or family gatherings, make it outdoors or make it small gatherings.
  • Be cautious when gathering with the elderly as COVID is is especially dangerous for the elderly
